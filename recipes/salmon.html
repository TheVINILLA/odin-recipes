Salmon Recipe

Baked, salted, and Tasty!

This method is dead simple, but so delicious and so good for you too. While all you really need for a satisfying piece of baked salmon is salt, pepper, and olive oil, itâ€™s easy to adapt to different herbs and ingredients.

This recipe in particular concentrates on lemon,garlic and butter and is one I personally use for a healthy and solid dinner.

Ingredients

  • salmon
  • 5 cloves of garlic
  • 2 pieces of lemon
  • 1 tsp of pepper
  • 1 tsp of salt
  • 42g of butter
  • 1/2 tsp of parsley

Steps

  1. Make marinade by mixing all applicable ingredients.
  2. Cut pieces of lemon.
  3. Marinate Salmon.
  4. Bake at 400 degrees for 28 minutes.
